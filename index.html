Time line and development summary of SpaceX:

  • 2002 - SpaceX was established and its testing period had started.
  • 2005 - The first failed launch of the Falcon I.
  • 2010 - The SpaceX Dragon was introduced. The first commercial spacecraft to be retrieved from orbit.
  • 2011 - SpaceX started a reusable program with the purpose of reusing an orbital launch system many times for their aircrafts.
  • 2012 - SpaceX becomes the first commercial company to dock with the International Space Station
  • 2015 - The private compnay sent a probe beyond the orbit of the Earth.
  • 2018 - The beginning of the Falcon Heavy launch, the most powerful rocket of the world.
  • 2020 - The first-ever crewed mission had been achieved.
